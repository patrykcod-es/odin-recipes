Tuna Pasta

Throw together some storecupboard ingredients and pop under the grill for a simple midweek meal

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta
  • Pesto Rosso
  • Canned tuna
  • Cherry tomato
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil pasta for 11 min until tender in salted water
  2. Drain the water leaving few spoons of pasta water
  3. Add halved cherry tomatoes
  4. Add drained tuna and mix it all together
