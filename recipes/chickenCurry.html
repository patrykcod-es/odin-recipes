Chicken Curry

This easy staple chicken curry is a fantastic recipe for family dinners. It's made with just a handful of ingredients and is enriched with creamy yogurt

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Curry paste
  • Tomato pulpa
  • Cumin
  • Yoghurt

Steps

  1. Fry chicken
  2. Add curry paste
  3. Add spieces
  4. Add tomato pulpa and cook for 20 min
  5. Stir in youghurt and serve
